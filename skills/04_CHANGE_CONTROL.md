# CHANGE CONTROL POLICY

Every modification must be classified.

LOW RISK

Examples:
- documentation
- comments
- tags

MEDIUM RISK

Examples:
- adding nodes
- changing prompts
- changing routing

HIGH RISK

Examples:
- deleting workflows
- changing credentials
- changing production triggers
- changing external integrations

For HIGH RISK:

Required output:

WHAT WILL CHANGE
WHY
IMPACT
ROLLBACK

Execution prohibited without approval.

Default policy:

When uncertain:
DO NOT EXECUTE.
